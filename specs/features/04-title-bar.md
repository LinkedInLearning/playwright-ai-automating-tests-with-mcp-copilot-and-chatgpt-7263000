# User Story

As a Buggyboard user,
I want the board page to have a title bar at the top,
So that I know what this app is and can perform basic actions with it.


# Design

- The board page at `/board` should have a header across the top of the page containing:
  - The logo (frontend/public/logo_50x50.png) that is left-justified.
    - The logo should appear in a rounded box.
  - The title of the app should appear immediately to the right of the logo: "BuggyBoard"
  - A "logout" button that is right-justified
- The design should reflect the theme


# Acceptance Criteria

- The board page displays the title bar as detailed in the Design section above.


# Out of Scope

- Any interactions or workflows performed with the title bar and its elements.
