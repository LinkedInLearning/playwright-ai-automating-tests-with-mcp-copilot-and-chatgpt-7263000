# Design theme

Visual and UI/UX design decisions for BuggyBoard. Use these when building or styling the app.

## Color

- **Primary color:** Volkswagen Savannah Beige – `#b8ae76`  
  Use as the main brand/accent color (buttons, links, highlights, etc.) where appropriate.

_(Other palette entries, neutrals, or semantic colors can be added here or in additional files under `specs/design/`.)_

## Modals

- **Consistent look and feel:** All modals in the app must share a consistent look and feel (e.g. same layout, header style, button placement, spacing, and use of the design theme).
- **Close control:** Every modal must have an **X** in the upper right corner. Clicking it closes the modal and has the same effect as the modal’s cancel action (e.g. close without saving).
- **Escape key:** Pressing the **Escape** key while a modal is open must have the same effect as the cancel action (e.g. close without saving).
